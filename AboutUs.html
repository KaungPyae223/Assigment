About Us

Our club has been founded since 1988. Our goal is that the Valley is the best place to meet together with those who have the hobby and interest of vintage rally cars. In 2022 we will have a total of 50k members all over the world. We make festivals, charity and events every year. We always make member meetings in November and we decide our future plans and events. In February, we always take a journey with our vintage rally cars across the countries.

Our Story

Our club has hounded since 1988. Our founder Robard John love driving and travelling around the country with his valley car. In 1988, when he was 30 years old, he had an idea to found a driving club and travel around the world with his friends. When the club was founded, it had five members and now it has over a ten thousand members all over the world.

Our Members

In 2022 our club has member over 10 thousands all over the world. Most of our members are from the European and North America. All the people who have a hobby of vintage rally car can join our club. Our club contains all the ages, gender and all people are kind and helpful. Our club has member community team and all the members can connect each other all the time easily.

Our events

We always celebrate the events in every July and Auguys of the year. In this event we drive our vintage rally car through four or five countries and we also visit to the famous places in these country. We also make festivale, vintage car show to give a knowledge about of vintage rally car and make a charity events. The purpose of makeing events is to meet our members each other and to get more friendship and happiness.

Our charity

Our club is also participant in many charity events. We make volunteers to other charity and we also make our charity events as we can. We always dontate our most of our member fees to the charity. We also participant environmental events, such as growing plants, stop huntig rare animals and stop throwing wastes to the oceans. During covid pendamic time, we donate and volunteers at hospital, clinic and quartine centers.
