Welcome to our vintage rally club

Our rally club always warmly welcomes our new Members and visitors. Our club has been founded since 1988. Our goal is that the Valley is the best place to meet together with those who have the hobby and interest of vintage rally cars. In 2022 we will have a total of 50k members all over the world. we make festivals, charity and shows. We went on a trip with our vintage rally car and met with our friends. During the covid pandemic time, we could not make a trip but in 2023 we have a plan to make a trip show and festivals. Thank you for joining our club.
